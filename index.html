Our App Idea:

We are going to build a Calendar application - one that works with an alarm for scheduling events. This will allow for scheduling events into the calendar on a time and day, with options for a description and an alarm. From there, it will also have a couple of display options: a day display detailing just a single day in one go, with the option to move to the day on either side, and a week display that displays the week of days side-by-side.
