Our App Idea:

We are going to build a Calendar/Timetable application - one that works with an alarm for scheduling events. This will allow for scheduling events into the calendar on a time and day, with options for a description and an alarm. From there, it will have several display options: a day display detailing just a single day in one go— an hourly display may also be useful—with the option to move to the day on either side, a week display that displays the week of days side-by-side, and of course a month display, to check which days currently have scheduled events or to select a new day to enter times into. We intend for this to be an all purpose app for any kind of calendar related usage, be it your university timetable, pill taking times, work schedule, one-off events etc.
